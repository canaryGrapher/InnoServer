Simple cloud solutions

We know hosting can be costly when your only source of income is recruitments. Let MAHE take care of your hosting needs, while you work on making your club awesome.

Virtual Machine

Deploy in just a few steps and you will be have a functional Linux VM to do as you please.

Database

If your project needs database access, we have set up a MongoDB on our server. You can grab your connection URI and get started.*

Storage

With our self-hosted NextCloud server, you get 1GB of storage on our server. Now, you can store all those essential receipts, MOUs, and Memos easily.

Serverless

Create your backend without actually coding with the help of AppWrite which helps you manage core backend needs with a set of easy-to-use and integrate REST APIs.*

Management Console

AutoKonsole is a all-in-one management console that will make managing your hosting plan with us a delight. Curently under development.*

Docker Hosting

If you already have a docker image, hosting it can be as simple as a right click. Our automation scripts will do all the hard work so that you don't have to.*

Conditions for use

This service is free, but it is bound by some rules that you need to understand and agree with before you go ahead with hosting your files, or using any of the services we provide.

Dos

  • If you are using your own SSL certificates, declare it beforehand. We provide SSL certificates by default to all hosted websites.
  • You need to let us know if you no longer need to the resources that were allocated to you so that we can free up those resources.
  • Notify downtine, or other issues to team as soon as posiible.
  • Give up extra resources so that others can utilize it.
  • Use up-to-date, stable packages on your VM
  • Keep your AutoConsole username and password secure*.

Don'ts

  • Do not run mail servers, or blockchain mining software on the server.
  • You are not allowed to do anything other than what you have got permission for. So you cannot host a mailing server on your VM which you got for hosting your webapp.
  • Activities or content that defames, harasses, discriminates against, harms or threatens other will be reported to the university authorities.
  • Don't host copyright material on your application. This is against the Innovation Center policy and if found in violation of this, your account will be suspended without any notice.
  • You are not allowed to use the server to fill your pockets. Doing so might cause action from the college administration.
  • Hosting on our servers does not mean that MAHE endorses or approves your website.
